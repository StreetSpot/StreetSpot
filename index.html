Data Deletion Instructions

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data from StreetSpot, please follow the steps below:

  • Send an email to support@yourdomain.com (or your Gmail).
  • Use the subject line: Request for Data Deletion.
  • Include your username or registered email address.

Your data will be permanently removed from our servers within 7 business days.
